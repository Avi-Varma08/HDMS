Patients Registry

The HDIMS Mobile Application gives patients a simple and safe way to access their health information,
follow their health progress, and connect with registered healthcare facilities. This app helps patients
by providing trustworthy health data, allowing them to make better decisions about their health and care.


Facility Registry

The Health Data Information & Management System (HDIMS) is a platform that helps healthcare facilities
easily manage and update health data. By using HDIMS, hospitals and healthcare departments at local,
district, and state levels can share health information smoothly and efficiently. This helps in accurately
monitoring healthcare activities and supports the effective implementation of health programs and policies.



Who Can Register?

Individuals, including patients who want healthcare services from registered public or private hospitals,
clinics, or diagnostic centers, can register on the HDIMS platform. This allows them to access a safe
system to manage their health information and stay updated on health programs.

Facility managers and administrative staff of hospitals, clinics, diagnostic labs, imaging centers, and
pharmacies, whether public or private, can also register to manage and update health data. By registering,
these facilities can be part of a connected health data system to improve healthcare services.
